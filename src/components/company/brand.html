Brand guidelines

Do use

  • Refer to Zod as "Zod" or "Zod AI"
  • Use the provided logo files
  • Maintain clear space around the logo
  • Use the correct color versions

Don't

  • Modify, rotate, or distort the logo
  • Use Zod in confusing product names
  • Claim endorsement without permission
  • Change the brand colors
