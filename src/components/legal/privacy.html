1. Personal data we collect

This Privacy Policy covers use of ZOD software, platform, APIs, documentation, and website. It does NOT apply where we act as data processor on behalf of commercial customers.

Collected directly: Account Information (name, email), Payment Information, Inputs and Suggestions, Communication Information, Feedback.

Collected automatically: Device Information, Log Information, Usage Data, Cookies and Similar Technologies, Location Information.

NOT collected: Sensitive/special category personal information (genetic, biometric, health, religious), children under 18.

We use your data to:

  • Improve and develop the Service

Model training: We do NOT use Inputs or Suggestions to train our models unless: (1) flagged for security review, (2) explicitly reported as Feedback, or (3) you have explicitly agreed.
