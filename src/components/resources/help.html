Frequently asked questions

How do I install Zod?

Visit our download page for platform-specific install instructions. Zod supports macOS, Linux, and Windows. You can also use package managers like Homebrew.

How do I reset or update my subscription?

You can manage your subscription from the Settings dashboard in the Zod app. For billing questions, contact our support team.

How do I enable Privacy Mode?

Privacy Mode can be enabled in Settings or by a team admin. When enabled, Zod will not train on your data. Learn more in our Data Use policy.

How do I contact support?

Email us at contact@zodagent.com or visit the community forum for help from other users.
